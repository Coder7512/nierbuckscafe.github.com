Our Doughnuts

In many nations, doughnuts are a type of ring-shaped snack item that is frequently deep-fried from flour dough. After being fried, doughnuts might be topped in powdered sugar, fruit, or sugar icing before being smeared with chocolate or frosting.

Simple pantry ingredients like flour, yeast, baking powder or soda, sugar, butter, and deep-frying oil are used to make doughnuts.
